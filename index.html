How It Works

This section helps customers understand the ordering process in just a few simple steps.

1

Send your request

The customer chooses a product from the showcase or sends a request for a specific item through the order form.

2

Confirmation and details

You review the request, confirm availability, price, and shipping details, then contact the customer directly.

3

Shipping and delivery

After confirmation, the order is processed and delivered using the method you agree on with the customer.

What you can show here

  • Shipping information and estimated delivery times
  • Payment methods
  • Return or exchange policy
  • Frequently asked questions

Why this works well

  • Customers get a clear and professional overview instead of scattered chat messages
  • You collect all important order details in one place
  • The website can later grow into a larger online store
  • It works well as your main link from eBay, social media, and messaging apps
