2. Scope of this Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect personal data when you:

  • use our website;
  • submit a request through our forms;
  • contact us by email or messenger;
  • communicate with us about a request, quotation, payment, or delivery.

3. What personal data we collect

Depending on how you interact with us, we may collect:

  • your name;
  • your email address or messenger/contact details;
  • the details you submit in a request form;
  • part name, OEM number, or part-related description;
  • uploaded photos or files;
  • vehicle-related information, such as brand, model, year, engine size, and transmission model;
  • the contents of your message or enquiry;
  • limited technical information necessary for website operation, security, and troubleshooting.

We do not intentionally collect special categories of personal data.

4. Why we use your personal data

We use personal data for the following purposes:

  • to review and process your request;
  • to identify requested parts and related product details;
  • to contact you about your enquiry;
  • to provide quotations or pre-contract information;
  • to communicate about availability, payment, shipping, or delivery;
  • to maintain business records and customer communication history;
  • to protect our website and services from misuse, spam, or abuse;
  • to comply with legal and regulatory obligations.

If we later introduce marketing communications, we may also use contact details to send promotional messages where permitted by applicable law and, where required, based on your consent.

5. Legal bases for processing

We process your personal data on one or more of the following legal bases:

  • to take steps at your request before entering into a contract, for example when you send a part request or ask for a quotation;
  • for our legitimate interests, including customer communication, business administration, handling enquiries efficiently, and protecting our website and services;
  • to comply with legal obligations, where applicable;
  • based on your consent, where consent is required, including for certain future marketing communications if introduced.

6. How long we keep your data

We keep personal data only for as long as necessary for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy.

As a general rule:

  • request and enquiry data may be kept for up to 12 months after the last relevant interaction;
  • transaction-related records may be kept longer where required for accounting, tax, legal, or compliance purposes;
  • uploaded files and photos may be deleted earlier if they are no longer needed to process your request.

Where necessary, we may retain information for longer to establish, exercise, or defend legal claims.
